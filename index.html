BleachHack

BleachHack is a utility mod for Minecraft originally made for endcrystal.me in 2019, it was one of the first 1.14+ utility mods and was a big part of kickstarting the Fabric and open-source era of Minecraft clients.

BleachHack has over 250k downloads and 590k Git changes.

Maintained: 2019-2023

Maintained: 2022

McOSM

A Mod that imports OpenStreetMap data into Minecraft, fully compatible with CubicChunks, Terra121 and the Build The Earth map projection.

Created for BuildTheEarth if it wasn't obvious.

Maintained: 2020-2022
